friend, rather than the things you know, well then you're always on a quest in a sense.
You're always looking for new information in the off chance that somebody who doesn't agree
with you will tell you something you couldn't have figured out on your own! It's a completely
different way of looking at the world. It's the antithesis of opinionated.
